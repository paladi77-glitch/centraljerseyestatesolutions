Why an Inheritance Advance?

America's courts are hopelessly backlogged. A probate cash advance can relieve the financial pressure, making it possible for heirs to preserve and enhance the value of estate property by providing funds to make needed repairs or prevent a foreclosure. A probate cash advance can also relieve the credit pressure on an executor or personal representative who must otherwise advance funds from their own accounts for estate related expenses.

How an Inheritance Advance is Different Than a Loan

  • Many heirs, facing stress connected with the backlog of the probate courts, often look for loans.
  • We provide inheritance advances which have NO monthly payments of any kind.
  • An inheritance advance is NOT a loan. It has no interest fees, no fixed due date, and imposes no personal recourse or liability in the event of a payment default.
  • Eligibility isn't dependent on your credit score or income.
  • Approval and the amount of your cash advance depend on your state of residence, the amount of your inheritance, and the assets of the estate.
  • After you, your inheritance cash advance is fully guaranteed and backed by your share in the estate. We simply wait for the estate to close and receive payment directly for the portion of your inheritance you assigned to us. We take the risk of any delay.
